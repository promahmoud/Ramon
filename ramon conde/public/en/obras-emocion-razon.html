EMOTION REASON

Go Back
Exhibition held at the Hejduk Towers of the City of Culture of Santiago de Compostela. On the outside, there is an individual that emerges from the earth, interrogating another person's gaze, forming an anthropomorphic portico that, through his arms, creates the entrance to the interior of the towers. Both characters, the one that requests and the one that gives access, are the same. The scene represents a kind of emotional immersion, an exercise of introspection, which allows us to visualize the emotional world that develops inside.

In the first tower, the visitor will meet a meeting of characters who listen to a leader, creator of an intellectual reality that materializes for them. It consists of a large head, symbolizing their beliefs, and two suspended figures, one on the right and one on the left -representing that things can be one way or their opposite.

In the second tower, the sample offers a series of small montages that represent different attitudes in the interpersonal relationship, reflecting the emotional influence of some individuals over others. Psychological power as the determining force of our social behavior.

Reaffirming this introspective act, the different actors are repeated in different situations and interpreting different roles. The images are reduced to heads as a synthesis of their emotional moments.

First of all it is a reflection on the role of conviction: how we are able to create parallel realities that are more determinant than the physical world.
